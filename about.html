About Our Ministry

Transforming lives through the power of God's Word

Our Story

The Sons of God Ministry was founded in 1995 with a vision to create a place where people from all walks of life could experience God's love and grow in their faith journey.

Through the years, we've remained committed to our founding principles of authentic worship, Biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach. As we've grown, we've expanded our mi[...]

Today, The Sons of God Ministry continues to be a place where lives are transformed through the power of God's Word and the love of His people.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as the Son of God who died for our sins and rose again.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who empowers believers to live godly lives.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, grow, and serve.

Service

We believe in serving others as Christ served us, meeting spiritual & practical needs.

Missions

We believe in the Great Commission and are committed to sharing the Gospel globally.
